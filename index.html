Welcome to real time weather app, your ultimate weather companion! Whether you're planning a weekend getaway, a daily commute, or outdoor activities, our app provides real-time weather updates tailored just for you.Get precise hourly and daily forecasts powered by advanced meteorological data.


No matter where you are, stay informed with weather updates from around the globe.Use the search bar to find a location and access detailed weather data, including current temperatures, humidity, wind speed, atmospheric pressure, sun rise and sunset. You can also select your preferred temperature unit, Celsius or Fahrenheit using the dropdown menu.
